Credi-Can is a recycling rewards platform where users bring recyclable materials (plastic bottles, metal, cans, glass) to physical kiosks, earn credits based on material type and quantity, and redeem those credits for rewards (bakery vouchers, transport, mobile airtime, education supplies, groceries).

Target Audience

Primary: Urban and semi-urban Cameroon residents with access to recycling kiosks

Secondary: Kiosk managers (admin users) who process waste submissions

Tertiary: Local businesses partnering as reward providers
